## What it is

A product platform I'm building quietly — details deliberately thin while it's
prototype-stage. The short version: a tool for operators who want what I want, in a
space where the existing options feel clumsy.

## Why

gardens. The interesting bit is building something small enough to ship, focused enough
to actually help, and honest enough that the people using it can fork their data out
whenever they want.
