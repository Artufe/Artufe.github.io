status: prototype

What it is

A product platform I'm building quietly — details deliberately thin while it's prototype-stage. The short version: a tool for operators who want what I want, in a space where the existing options feel clumsy.

Why

I've spent a decade watching the same problems get solved again inside different walled gardens. The interesting bit is building something small enough to ship, focused enough to actually help, and honest enough that the people using it can fork their data out whenever they want.

Where it's at now

Status: prototype. Usable by me; nothing ready for external hands yet. Growing quietly, without the launch-day hype cycle.

What's next

  • Refine the one workflow that matters most and get it bulletproof.
  • Invite a small handful of early users — people I know, who'll actually use it and give honest feedback.
  • Ship publicly when the experience is boring in a good way.

How to follow

No newsletter. If you want to know when it opens up, email me and I'll add you to a small list.
